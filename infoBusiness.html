BUSINESS STRATEGY

Strategy and planning for businesses both big and small
Whether you are starting your first business or looking at winding up, we want to work with you to help build your business with firm foundations and reduce the pressures of running a business.

Throughout all stages of business, we want to work with you to ensure that your business is established with firm foundations that will give it that strength and consistency it needs to succeed long into the future.

While primarily servicing Hervey Bay and the Fraser Coast, we work with clients Australia wide to provide assistance with:

  • Due diligence when purchasing a business or asset to reduce risk and identify opportunities.
  • Effective business structuring to maximise asset protection and minimise your taxes.
  • Setting up sole traders, partnerships, companies, and trusts.
  • Helping to establish efficient accounting and record keeping procedures to save you time and money.
  • Organising and storing business documents.
  • Applying for all relevant registrations (such as TFN, Business Name, ABN, GST, PAYGW, PAYGI, FBT, ACN, Payroll Tax).
  • Company services including Annual ASIC Statements, changing of directors or company details, transfers of shares.
  • Business mentoring along the way to provide advice around strategic decision-making.
  • Copyrights, Trademarks and Intellectual Property advice.
  • Assisting in the preparation of finance applications.
  • Queensland Building & Construction Commission (QBCC) reporting and advice.
  • Preparing your business for sale or winding up.
