ACCOUNTING

Accounting and taxation services for both business and individuals
We provide tailored accounting services for all entity types to ensure you meet your legal obligations and minimise tax.

We help individuals, businesses and investors throughout Hervey Bay and Australia wide, meet their legal obligations and maximise their profits by minimising their tax. We are passionate about educating our clients on how to reduce their tax exposure and equipping them with strategies and tools to work and trade with confidence.

Make use of our extensive experience preparing end of year Tax Returns and Financial Statements for Individuals, Sole traders, Partnerships, Trusts, Companies, Self-Managed Super Funds (SMSF). We provide clients with expert advice for the following areas of Taxation Law:

  • Capital Gains Tax (CGT) implications and concessions available for the purchase and sale of investments including property, shares, managed funds & other similar investments
  • Crypto Trading Schedules
  • Rental Schedules for residential and commercial properties including Airbnb’s, caravan parks, motels & hostels
  • Depreciation schedules
  • Fringe Benefit Tax (FBT) and concessions available for employees of charities, and public benevolent institutions (PBI)
  • Research & Development (R&D) tax incentives
  • Primary Production income and concessions (crops, graziers, fisheries and forestry)
  • Special Professional income
We also provide a range of auxiliary services including:
  • Business Activity Statements (BAS) and associated requirements such as Goods & Services Tax (GST), Pay As You Go Withholding (PAYGW), Pay As You Go Instalments (PAYGI), Fringe Benefits Tax (FBT), Fuel Tax Credits (FTC), Luxury Car Tax (LCT) and Wine Equalisation Tax (WET)
  • Tax Planning and effective Business Structuring
  • Deceased Estates and Estate Planning
  • Corresponding with the ATO including preparing assessment objections and applying for private rulings
  • Annual FBT Returns
  • Transfer Balance Account Reporting (TBAR) for SMSFs
  • Queensland Building & Construction Commission (QBCC) annual reporting and advice
  • International Taxation
  • Review and Amendment of prior year tax returns
If you have a tax problem giving you drama or you’re looking for an Accountant with a more personalised service, please feel free to contact us. We look forward to working with you.
